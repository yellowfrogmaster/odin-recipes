Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for making a pizza called Heavy Heaven. It is a large pizza with many good ingredients that will take you on a culinary trip around the world.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Pepperoni
  • Beef
  • Meatballs

Steps:

  1. Make dough
  2. Spread tomatu sauce on dough
  3. Spread the cheese on top
  4. Put on ham, beef, meatballs and pepperoni
  5. Top it off with your favorite vegetables
